## Submit your example

Open a PR adding your project to this directory:

```
examples/
└── your-project-name/
    ├── README.md          # what you built, what Genesis generated
    ├── manifest.md        # your .genesis/manifest.md
    └── architecture/      # your .genesis/architecture/ (optional)
```
